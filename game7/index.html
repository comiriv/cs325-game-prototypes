    • The player will receive a roll at the beginning of their turn that says how many spaces they can move. Pressing the SPACE button will move them in a clockwise faction till their roll is 0. Along the way, if they pass a plane, bus, or train station, they can press ENTER to use the station provided that it has been unused and they can afford it (Bus - 100, Train - 150, Plane - 200). If the player lands on a nontrain station, they will receive the reward or consequence of that space (Either money or they get robbed). After that the next player's turn starts with his roll. This process continues for 15 rounds or until all the plane and bus stations are used up.
